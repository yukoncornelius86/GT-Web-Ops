What this service is

Undercarriage preservation protects the overlooked side of the vehicle: exposed underbody areas, frame sections, suspension areas, seams, and common corrosion-prone zones. The treatment is designed to creep into places hard coatings may miss while staying flexible over time.

For vehicles with heavy buildup underneath, dry ice cleaning may be the right preparation step before preservation.

Good fit for

  • Daily drivers
  • Trucks and SUVs
  • Classic and enthusiast vehicles
  • Vehicles kept long term
  • Coastal or rural driving environments

Flat-rate pricing

$549 undercarriage preservation for most vehicles.

What it is not

This is not structural rust repair, it does not reverse existing corrosion, and it is not a permanent lifetime coating. It is a careful preservation service designed to help reduce future corrosion exposure.

Will it drip or smell?

Some minor weeping or odor can occur shortly after application. We apply carefully to reduce unnecessary mess while still getting useful coverage.

The flat rate covers most vehicles. If heavy mud, severe rust scale, old coatings, or unusual access will change the work, we review that before service.
