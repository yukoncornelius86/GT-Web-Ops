Built for Southern Maryland drivers who want the work done carefully.

The GT Collective is a Leonardtown detailing studio serving customers who are searching for ceramic coating, paint correction, paint polishing, and careful automotive detailing near St. Mary's County. Vehicles come in from the Pax River corridor, Lexington Park, California, Hollywood, Great Mills, Mechanicsville, Lusby, Solomons, and nearby Southern Maryland communities.

Every correction or coating job starts with an inspection. We explain what the paint actually needs, what can realistically improve, and which service makes sense before any work begins.

Our Story

Built by
Mike Zanni

U.S. Navy Veteran Husband & Father Craftsman Founder
"I did not build this to be just another service business. I built it because I believe the details matter: in vehicles, in coffee, and in how we show up for the people we serve."

The GT Collective was born from a transition: from military service to something new, but no less deliberate. After retiring from the Navy, I wanted to build something rooted in the same values: precision, integrity, and care.

Every vehicle we detail, every bag of coffee we ship, and every customer we serve comes from that same place: the belief that the journey deserves attention.
