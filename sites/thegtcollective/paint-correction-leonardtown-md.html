Correction first

Ceramic coating locks in the finish underneath it.

If the paint is cloudy or swirled before coating, the coating will preserve that look. Paint polishing is the prep step that makes the finish worth protecting.

Common questions

Will paint polishing make my car look new?

It can dramatically improve gloss and clarity, but the final result depends on paint thickness, defect depth, prior damage, and how much clear coat can safely be polished.

Do you offer paint polishing near Lexington Park and Pax River?

Yes. The Leonardtown studio serves St. Mary's County, Lexington Park, California, Great Mills, Hollywood, and the NAS Patuxent River corridor.

What does paint correction start at?

Paint correction starts at $600. Final pricing depends on the vehicle, condition, target result, and whether coating prep is included.
