What this service is

Dry ice cleaning uses pressurized CO2 pellets to lift contamination from surfaces without leaving water or chemical residue. It is useful for collector vehicles, specialty vehicles, undercarriage cleanup, and coating or preservation prep.

Pricing

Dry ice cleaning is quoted by consultation because access, contamination level, and vehicle condition vary widely.

Why it is different

Unlike pressure washing or solvent-heavy cleaning, dry ice cleaning does not soak the vehicle or leave product behind. It is a precise tool for jobs where careful removal matters more than speed.

Common questions

Does dry ice cleaning use water?

No. The CO2 media sublimates, which means it turns from solid to gas and does not leave water behind.

Is it right for every vehicle?

No. We inspect first and explain whether dry ice cleaning, conventional detailing, or another prep method makes more sense.
