What this service is

Ceramic coating is a protective layer applied over properly prepared paint. It helps water bead, makes routine washing easier, and adds gloss after the paint has been corrected. The coating is not a substitute for preparation, so The GT Collective treats correction and surface prep as part of the job rather than an optional afterthought.

Common questions

Is ceramic coating worth it in Southern Maryland?

It can be, especially if you want easier washing and long-term protection against regular road film, rain, pollen, and coastal humidity. It works best when applied after proper paint correction.

Is ceramic coating the same as wax?

No. A ceramic coating is a longer-lasting protective coating. Wax is easier to apply but does not provide the same durability or chemical resistance.
